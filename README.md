# 🚀 Complete ERP + POS + CRM System

A full-featured, all-in-one business management solution built with the MERN stack. Perfect for retail stores, restaurants, e-commerce, and SMEs.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🛒 Point of Sale (POS)
- Intuitive product search (Name/Barcode/QR)
- Multi-payment support: Cash, Card, Mobile (bKash/Nagad)
- Instant receipt printing
- Cart management & discount options
- Price levels (Retail, Wholesale, VIP)

### 👥 Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
- Lead management (Lead → Customer → VIP → VVIP)
- Customer segmentation & filtering
- Follow-up reminder system
- Source tracking & conversation logs
- Customer history tracking

### 📊 Real-time Dashboard
- Sales analytics & interactive charts
- Payment distribution (Pie & Bar charts)
- Low stock alerts
- Profit/Loss overview
- Today's sales & transactions

### 📦 Inventory Management
- Product & stock tracking
- Low stock notifications
- Purchase management
- Stock value calculation
- Barcode/SKU management

### 📈 Financial Reports
- Profit & Loss Statement
- Balance Sheet & Trial Balance
- Daily, Monthly & Annual reports
- Cash settlement
- Ledger & transaction history

### 📱 Additional Features
- Fully mobile responsive design
- Crypto invoice generation
- JWT Authentication
- Data export capabilities
- QR code scanner
- Multi-company support

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React.js, Tailwind CSS, Recharts, Heroicons
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
- **Database:** MongoDB
- **Authentication:** JWT
- **QR Scanner:** html5-qrcode
